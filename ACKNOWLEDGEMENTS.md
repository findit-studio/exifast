# Acknowledgements

`exifast` exists only because of **Phil Harvey** and the
[**ExifTool**](https://exiftool.org/) project — over two decades of
meticulously reverse-engineered file-format and metadata knowledge.

`exifast` is an independent Rust port of ExifTool. It is not affiliated with
or endorsed by Phil Harvey. All format knowledge derives from ExifTool's
source and documentation; every ported module references its ExifTool origin.

Test fixtures are reused from ExifTool's own `t/images/` test corpus.

Thank you to Phil Harvey and all ExifTool contributors.
